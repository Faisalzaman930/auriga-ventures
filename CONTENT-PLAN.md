## Content Format & Voice

**Tone:** Confident, editorial, first-person. Write as someone who has *been there*, not someone who read about it.

**Structure for destination posts:**
1. Opening image + one-sentence emotional hook
2. "What makes this place different" — 2–3 paragraphs
3. "What Auriga does here" — specific, concrete, no vague claims
4. Practical section (best time, how to get there, what to pack)
5. Pull quote from a past client
6. CTA: "Ready to go? Let's build your journey."

**Never:**
- Generic bullet lists ("Top 10 things to do in…")
- Stock photos
- Filler content written to fill word count

**Always:**
- Real photos from actual Auriga trips
- Specific details (names of places, names of stays, real experiences)
- One clear CTA per post → WhatsApp or enquiry form
