Voice & Format

How Every Post Should Read

Tone: confident, editorial, first-person. Write as someone who has been there, not someone who read about it.

✓
Specific details. Names of actual stays, real experiences, exact viewpoints. Vague claims destroy trust.
✓
Real photos. Only Auriga's own trip photography. Stock photos signal inauthenticity instantly.
✓
One CTA per post. WhatsApp or enquiry form — not both, not buried. End every piece with it.
✓
Client pull quotes. One real quote per destination post. Proof beats persuasion.
✗
No generic listicles. "Top 10 things to do in Hunza" — this is what every competitor writes. Don't compete there.
✗
No filler. Every paragraph should earn its place. If it doesn't inform or persuade, cut it.
